Book Go Round

Book-Go-Round is operated by volunteers of the Friends of the Saratoga Libraries. All proceeds benefit The Saratoga library in Saratoga, CA, a Santa Clara County Library.

You never know what treasures will turn up on our shelves. Our inventory is constantly changing with everything from barely used current titles, to book club favorites, to collectibles and gorgeous art books all at great prices.

Browse through literature, beach reads, history, travel, culture, art, foreign language, children's books, audio books, sheet music, CDs, DVDs, art prints, and more at any of our three locations.
